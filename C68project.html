What's New

  • New School Auditorium

    Our school has built a new auditorium in which all the functions can be organised. It is well-built, ventilated and spacious. We are thankful to our Principal for bulding this wonderful auditorium.

  • Online Records Submission

    Our school management has introduced an Online Record Submission system, so that, the records submitted by every student are stored online and can be easily accessed whenever necessary. The best part of this is, these records can be used as reference for juniors. But students have to be careful that, they have to submit the records in the correct format and before the mentioned date.
